CLADLITE™

Innovation in composite cladding.

CLADLITE™ is a fire-rated, decorative, rigid, lightweight composite panel ideal for architectural cladding and re-cladding of building façades. A huge range of striking, durable finishes — natural stone, stone effect, wood, concrete — at a fraction of the weight and cost of traditional materials, with better thermal insulation and sound absorption.

Far superior in impact and damage resistance versus ceramic / porcelain, natural stone, and ACM. Available as both flat and three-dimensional panel designs with finishes that can match the style of any exterior — from contemporary to classic.

REFURBISHMENT

Lightweight cladding systems for re-cladding existing buildings.

CLADLITE™ is the ideal solution for breathing new life into tired or outdated façades. Lightweight yet rigid, high-performance construction enables stunning aesthetic transformations without extensive structural modification — a practical and cost-effective route to modernisation.

  • Enhanced aesthetics — refresh and modernise outdated façades with contemporary finishes and textures.
  • Improved fire safety — non-combustible or fire-resistant systems that meet the latest regulations.
  • Better insulation & efficiency — ventilated systems with modern insulation reduce energy costs.
  • Minimal structural disruption — easily retrofitted without major structural changes.
  • Increased property value — modern, compliant exteriors enhance market appeal.
SUSTAINABILITY AT THE HEART OF INNOVATION

Built for the long term.

CLADLITE™ is engineered to significantly reduce carbon emissions when compared to traditional cladding materials. From sourcing of raw materials to manufacturing, transportation, and installation — every stage is optimised to minimise environmental impact. Its lightweight composition lowers transport fuel and simplifies installation, reducing energy use and labour requirements on site.

The product's unique durability and non-porous surface make it highly resistant to weathering and degradation, requiring virtually no maintenance over its lifespan. In the rare event of damage, it is easily repairable — extending usability, reducing waste, and aligning with circular-economy principles.

At BFG, sustainability is a foundational principle that drives our design philosophy and product development.
